spacelamp creates and cares for places that do good in the world

we’re focused on making spaces and processes that enable the people and organisations creating social and ecological justice to thrive in their work and play

services

placemaking we provide comprehensive placemaking and community development services based in real world experience creating loved places in whangārei and te whanganui-a-tara

facilitation experienced facilitator using techniques based in design thinking, collaborative practices, and empowering group processes

design collaborative design processes from space to brand, product and event.

projects

two/fiftyseven

a place of caring, connection and creativity for people to work and play together towards a socially just and sustainable future. two/fiftyseven is a work, meeting and event space, within a hub of action and care, we’re creating inclusive, community-focused impact by making space accessible and bringing people together.

founded in 2021, in the first three years of operation the space has hosted over 1,100 events, provided space for over 600 organisations, donated over $230,000 of space and provided a home for over 250,000 hours of work and play.

oneonesix

we supported the founding of whangārei’s leading community led arts space. a passionate group took over an unused central city former church owned by the council, which now hosts 20,000 visitors a year, is home to our community radio station, and is a driver of high quality artistic development in tai tokerau

oneonethree

a small coworking, events and residential space in te whanganui a tara hosting small impact organisations, residencies of travelling practitioners, and gigs featuring some of the finest young musicians in aotearoa
